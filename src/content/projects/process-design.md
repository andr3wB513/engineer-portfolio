## What I do

Chemical process design for plants: taking a process from concept to something that can
be built, operated, and maintained safely. I'm a licensed PE (South Carolina), and I
practice this at <a href="https://www.totalaspectdesign.com">Total Aspect Design</a>, a
small process design company in Greer, SC.

## The work

- **Piping system design per ASME B31.3** — line sizing, material selection, thermal
  expansion/flexibility analysis, and pressure relief design
- **Instrument specification packages** — datasheets ready for competitive vendor
  bidding, through technical bid evaluation and procurement
- **PFD & P&ID development** — mass-and-energy-balance-driven process flow diagrams and
  P&IDs, carried through HAZOP and design review cycles
